## üë§ Personal Accounts

### What is a Personal Account?

A personal account lets you create your **virtual self** - an AI-powered digital twin that represents you when you're not available.

### Setting Up Your Virtual Self

#### Step 1: Complete Your Profile

After onboarding, go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Training**.

Fill in the following sections:

**Identity**
- Full Name
- Birth Date & Place
- Current Location
- Languages you speak
- Bio/About yourself
- Relationship status
- Hobbies
- Favorite food

**Education**
- Schools attended
- Degrees earned
- Graduation years
- Special achievements

**Experience**
- Job titles and companies
- Duration of employment
- Key responsibilities
- Notable projects

**Skills**
- Technical skills
- Soft skills
- Certifications
- Expertise areas

**Projects**
- Personal projects
- Professional work
- Open source contributions
- Creative endeavors

**Social Links**
- LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub
- Personal website
- Portfolio links

#### Step 2: Train Your AI

1. Click **"Train My Virtual Self"**
2. Wait for training to complete (usually 10-30 seconds)
3. Your AI is now ready!

#### Step 3: Share Your Virtual Self

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings**
2. Enable **"Public Sharing"**
3. Choose a custom slug (e.g., `velamini.com/chat/yourname`)
4. Share the link with friends, colleagues, or on social media

### Using Your Virtual Self

**Chat with Yourself**
- Test how your AI responds
- Refine training data based on responses
- Update personality traits

**Share with Others**
- Others can chat with your virtual self 24/7
- Great for networking, FAQs, and availability
- Your virtual self represents you authentically

**Update Anytime**
- Go back to Training page
- Update any information
- Re-train your AI
- Changes take effect immediately

## üè¢ Organization Accounts

### What is an Organization Account?

Organization accounts enable businesses to provide **AI-powered customer support** via WhatsApp - without any technical setup.

### Setting Up Your Organization

#### Step 1: Create Organization

1. During onboarding, select **"Organization Account"**
2. Enter:
   - Organization name
   - Industry (optional)
3. Click **"Continue"**

You'll be redirected to the Organizations dashboard.

#### Step 2: Provision a WhatsApp Number

1. Click on your organization
2. Click **"Provision a Number"**
3. Select country
4. Click **"Search Numbers"**
5. Choose a number from the list
6. Click **"Select"** to provision

**Note:** This will activate the number through Twilio. All infrastructure is managed by Velamini - you don't need a Twilio account!

#### Step 3: Train Your AI Assistant

1. Go to **"AI Training"** tab
2. Fill in your business information:
   - Business description
   - Products/services
   - Common questions & answers
   - Brand personality
   - Contact information
3. Click **"Train AI"**

#### Step 4: Configure Settings

**General Settings**
- Organization name
- Description
- Welcome message (sent to new customers)
- Auto-reply toggle

**Business Hours**
- Enable/disable business hours
- Set start time (e.g., 9:00 AM)
- Set end time (e.g., 5:00 PM)
- Choose timezone

Outside business hours, customers receive an automatic message.

### Using Your WhatsApp AI

#### For Your Customers

1. **Share Your WhatsApp Number**
   - Add to website
   - Include in email signatures
   - Print on business cards
   - Share on social media

2. **Customers Text Your Number**
   - They send a WhatsApp message
   - AI responds instantly 24/7
   - Conversations feel natural

3. **AI Responds Intelligently**
   - Uses your business knowledge
   - Answers common questions
   - Provides product information
   - Escalates complex queries

#### For You (Organization Owner)

**Monitor Usage**
- View message count
- Track conversations
- See usage percentage
- Get alerts when nearing limits

**View Analytics**
- Total conversations
- Total messages
- Recent conversations
- Response times

**Manage Settings**
- Update AI training
- Change welcome message
- Adjust business hours
- Edit organization profile

### Managing Your WhatsApp Number

#### Viewing Number Details

1. Go to Organizations Dashboard
2. Click on your organization
3. View current number in **Overview** tab

#### Editing Number Configuration

1. Go to **Settings** tab
2. Update:
   - Display name
   - Welcome message
   - Business hours
3. Click **"Save Settings"**

#### Changing Your Number

1. Go to Overview tab
2. Click **"Release Number"**
3. Confirm release
4. Search and provision a new number

**Warning:** Releasing a number means customers can no longer text it. Update all materials with your new number.

#### Usage Limits

Each plan has message limits:
- **Trial**: 100 messages/month
- **Starter**: 500 messages/month
- **Business**: 2,000 messages/month
- **Enterprise**: 10,000+ messages/month

When you reach your limit:
- New messages receive a "limit reached" notification
- Upgrade your plan to continue
- Limits reset monthly

### Plan Upgrades

To upgrade your plan:
1. Go to **Settings** tab
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Choose new plan
4. Complete payment
5. New limits apply immediately

## ‚ùì FAQs

### General

**Q: Is Velamini free?**
A: Personal accounts are free forever. Organizations start with a free trial, then paid plans.

**Q: How secure is my data?**
A: All data is encrypted, stored securely, and never shared. See our [Privacy Policy](https://velamini.com/privacy).

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, go to Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. All data is permanently removed.

### Personal Accounts

**Q: How do I make my virtual self more accurate?**
A: Provide detailed information in all training sections. The more you share, the better!

**Q: Can I limit who can chat with my virtual self?**
A: Currently, shared virtual selves are public. Private sharing is coming soon!

**Q: What languages does my virtual self support?**
A: Add languages in your profile. AI will automatically respond in the language used.

### Organization Accounts

**Q: Do I need a Twilio account?**
A: No! Velamini handles all Twilio infrastructure for you.

**Q: Can I use my existing WhatsApp Business number?**
A: Not yet. You'll provision a new number through Velamini. We're working on number porting.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my message limit?**
A: New messages receive a notification. Upgrade to continue service.

**Q: Can I have multiple WhatsApp numbers?**
A: Yes! Enterprise plans support multiple numbers across organizations.

**Q: How do I escalate to a human?**
A: Configure keywords (e.g., "speak to human") to send you notifications. Coming soon!

**Q: Can I customize the AI personality?**
A: Yes! Train your AI with your brand voice, tone, and style.

**Q: What if a customer asks something the AI doesn't know?**
A: AI will politely say it doesn't know and offer to connect them with support.

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Personal Account Issues

**Issue: My virtual self isn't responding correctly**

**Solutions:**
1. Go to Training page
2. Review and update information
3. Re-train your AI
4. Test again

**Issue: Shared link not working**

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure sharing is enabled in Settings
2. Check your slug is unique
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support

### Organization Account Issues

**Issue: Can't provision a number**

**Solutions:**
1. Check country is supported
2. Try different area code
3. Try different country
4. Contact support if persistent

**Issue: AI not responding to WhatsApp messages**

**Solutions:**
1. Verify number is provisioned
2. Check AI is trained
3. Ensure organization is active
4. Check message limits
5. View Recent Conversations for errors

**Issue: Customers not receiving responses**

**Solutions:**
1. Check business hours settings
2. Verify auto-reply is enabled
3. Check message limits
4. Contact support

**Issue: Usage stats not updating**

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Check Recent Conversations
3. Wait a few minutes for sync
4. Contact support if persistent
